Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

EIT LLC ("EIT," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit eitllc.org or contact us through this site, how we use it, and how you can reach us with questions.

Information We Collect

We collect only what you choose to give us:

  • Contact form submissions. Your name, email address, and any other details you include in the form (company, phone, website, area of interest, budget range, and message).
  • Email correspondence. If you email us directly, we receive whatever you send.
  • Basic technical data. Standard server and browser information such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This may be collected by our hosting provider for security and operational purposes.

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and discuss whether we can help.
  • Provide consulting services if we begin an engagement.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve our website.
  • Comply with applicable laws and protect our legal rights.

We do not sell your information. We do not send marketing emails unless you specifically request them.

When We Share Information

We share information only when necessary, and only with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the site or deliver our services (for example, email, hosting, and form delivery providers), under confidentiality terms.
  • Legal authorities when required by law, subpoena, or to protect rights, property, or safety.

How Long We Keep It

We keep contact submissions and project communications only for as long as needed to respond, deliver work, and meet legal or accounting obligations. We delete or anonymize information when it is no longer needed.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you, and to object to or restrict certain uses. To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@eitllc.org and we will respond within a reasonable time.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you send us. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we use modern, standard practices to safeguard your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date above will reflect any changes. Material updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email info@eitllc.org. EIT LLC is based in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, United States.
