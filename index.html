Who We Are

We build systems,
not slide decks.

EIT is a Pennsylvania-based IT consulting firm founded in 2022. We work with small and mid-size businesses that are tired of doing things the hard way and want systems that actually run.

We don't do generic recommendations or vendor-neutral advice that goes nowhere. We get into your operations, find what's broken or slow, and build the automation that fixes it.

Our specialty is n8n, modern integration platforms, and connecting practical tools to real business processes — not as a novelty, but as working infrastructure.

You Own What We Build

No proprietary platforms, no lock-in. Every workflow and integration is fully yours to modify, maintain, or hand to anyone else.

Built for Your Stage of Growth

Enterprise-grade automation doesn't require an enterprise budget. We start small, prove value fast, and build from there.

Practitioners, Not Consultants

We live inside these tools every day. You get someone who's already solved the problem you're describing, not someone researching it on your dime.

Outcomes Over Hours

We measure success by what changes in your operation, not by deliverables, meetings, or time spent on calls.
