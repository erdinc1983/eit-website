Who We Are

Your automation partner,
not just another vendor

EIT is a Pennsylvania-based IT consulting firm founded in 2022. We work with small and mid-size businesses to eliminate manual bottlenecks, connect disconnected tools, and build automated systems that run reliably in the background.

Most businesses don't need a massive IT overhaul. They need someone who understands their workflows, identifies where time and money are being wasted, and builds practical automation that pays for itself quickly.

We specialize in n8n, AI-powered tools, and modern integration platforms to create workflows that handle the repetitive work your team shouldn't be doing manually.

Built for Growing Businesses

Enterprise-grade automation shouldn't require an enterprise budget. We design systems that scale with your business — starting small, proving value fast, and expanding from there.

You Own Everything We Build

No vendor lock-in, no proprietary platforms. Every workflow, integration, and system we create is fully yours to own, modify, and maintain indefinitely.

What We Do

Services built for
real business problems

We focus on practical, high-ROI automation that eliminates busywork and connects your tools into one smooth operation.

n8n Workflow Automation

Custom n8n workflows that connect your apps, automate repetitive tasks, and move data between systems without manual effort — from lead routing to invoice processing.

AI-Powered Workflows

Integrating AI tools into your business processes — for smart document handling, email triage, content generation, classification, and decision support.

Systems Integration

Connecting CRMs, ERPs, accounting tools, marketing platforms, and custom databases into a unified system that eliminates double-entry and data silos.

Process Consulting

Technical Advisory

Managed Automation

Ongoing monitoring, maintenance, and optimization of your automated systems. We keep your workflows running smoothly so you don't have to think about them.
